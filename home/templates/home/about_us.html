WHO ARE WE?

SH Furniture Udhyog is contributing in its field for more than 8 years till now. Previously, its name was termed as SH Steels Udhyog. The furniture products available in SH include; Sofa, Chair, Table, Daraz, Cupboard, and so on along with other household, official and hospital furniture items.

Moreover, the furniture products repairing facility is also provided by the company. The products produced by this company are reliable, trustworthy and durable which certainly satisfies the needs and desires of the clients.

Why us

MISSION & VISION

In context of Nepal, as we observe that there is a trend of importing a lot of furniture products from abroad. So, being a producer of such products, its the duty of our (SH Furniture) to take a step and eliminate such trend. SH Furniture Udhyog is giving continuity to set a fine example in being first to eradicate such trend. They also believe in marketing their own home products to each and every corner of the nation.

The major focus of SH Furniture Udhyog is to fluorish the nepali furniture items and make people convinced that our nepali products can also be qualitative with affordable rates.
